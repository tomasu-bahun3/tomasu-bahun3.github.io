A bit about me!

Hello, my name is Tom! You can find some examples of my academic work on the home page to get a sense of my Java abilities and skills. However, here I'll tell you a little about myself.

Having graduated with a bachelor's degree in Political Science and History from the University of Southern Maine in 2015, I traveled to Japan to learn the language and pursue a career in international relations. However, I quickly found myself steering toward a more technical career path, and in 2018 I returned home and found employment as a digital content manager for a local nonprofit.

I currently manage our website, databases, and tracking systems, while implementing solutions to IT issues. In addition, I am the organization's lead for cyber security training and ensuring that deliverables are met.

Last year, I enrolled at Southern Maine Community College in the computer science and cyber security programs to further my studies and skills with advice from friends and colleagues working as developers. Here I have gained experience with Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and more, while making use of Unified Modeling Language (UML), collaborative tools/version control (such as Git), and much more. I have learned a lot and have an expected graduate date of December 2020.

It is my hope to find employment as a Software Developer and progress my career to the next level.
